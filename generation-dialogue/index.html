Generation Dialogue

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

Generation Dialogue is a facilitation support tool developed in partnership with Elman Peace Centre and the GIZ FGM Prevention Programme. It helps community dialogue champions facilitate Generation Dialogue sessions.

Data Collection

Generation Dialogue does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information you enter — your name, organization, session history, and share-back reports — is stored locally on your device only.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or location information.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not connect to any third-party services. It functions entirely offline after installation.

Data Storage

All data is stored on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for adult community facilitators. It is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date.
