About the App

Generation Dialogue is a facilitation companion for community dialogue champions working within the GIZ FGM Prevention Programme. It provides real-time scenario guidance, technique recommendations, and session share-back reporting.

Getting Help

For support with the app, contact your program coordinator or reach out to us directly:

digital@elmanpeace.org

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored online?

No. All data stays on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server.

How do I switch languages?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and select your preferred language. The app supports English and Somali.

How do I report a bug?

Email digital@elmanpeace.org with a description of what happened and we will address it.
