Mind Trust

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

Mind Trust is a peacebuilding coordination platform developed by Elman Peace Centre. It supports field workers, facilitators, and program coordinators in managing sessions, stories, reflections, and field reports.

Data Collection

Mind Trust connects to a secure backend server to enable coordination between team members. The following data is collected and stored on our servers:

Account information (name, email, role), session records, field reports, stories, reflections, and feedback submitted through the app.

Data Use

Your data is used solely for program coordination and reporting within the Elman Peace Centre network. We do not sell, share, or provide your data to third parties.

Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Access to data is restricted by role-based permissions.

No Analytics or Advertising

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for the duration of your participation in the program. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date.
